Robotic Pets

A Introduction

The general area to be studied are the outcomes for people with mental illness and robot pet care. I believe this area is important because it can help communities find ways to improve mental health care for those who may not be able to care for a pet, but would benefit from interactions with a pet-like companion. This would be beneficial for neuroscientists, and members of the neuro-divergent community.
